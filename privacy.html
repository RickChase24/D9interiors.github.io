Privacy Policy - D9 Edge Construction Management App

Effective Date: June 10, 2025
Internal Employee App: This privacy policy applies to the D9 Edge construction management app, which is exclusively for Division 9 Interiors Inc. employees and authorized personnel.

Information We Collect

Account Information: The app requires only a password that you set during initial setup. Your email address is provided separately by company administration and is not collected through the app.

Work-Related Data: The app processes work-related information you enter, including:

  • Project details and progress updates
  • Budget and expense information
  • Crew assignments and scheduling data
  • Work site activities and reports

How We Use Information

All information is used exclusively for legitimate business purposes:

  • Project management and coordination
  • Budget tracking and financial reporting
  • Workforce planning and scheduling
  • Business operations and performance analysis

Information Sharing

Work-related data is accessible to:

  • Your direct supervisors and project managers
  • Company management as needed for business operations
  • IT personnel for technical support and system maintenance

We do not share information with third parties outside of Division 9 Interiors Inc.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes secure data transmission and storage protocols.

Data Retention

Work-related data is retained for business record-keeping purposes in accordance with company policies and applicable legal requirements.

Your Rights

As an employee, you have the right to:

  • Access your work-related information in the app
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Contact management with questions about data practices
